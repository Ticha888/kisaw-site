EDUCATION BRAND IDENTITY SOCIAL MEDIA CONCEPT
We created the visual identity for Abilka School, an educational space that inspires adults to explore design, illustration, and creativity through intensive programs and hands-on projects.

Abilka Space is a community. Space. Prostor. Пространство.

We wish to bring more opportunities to people to create, to learn, to work and to be happy.

Abilka Space is educational programs, intensive lessons and practical exercises for adults in the field of design, illustration and creativity.

We offer a variety of classes which include drawings and sketches, short courses, lecture hall meetings with joint projects, kids school, guest masters with their own programs and events with friends.
