NEW BRAND PACKAGE

A brand package is a complete foundation for a new brand — built from scratch, with a clear strategy and a visual language that works from day one.

It gives your business a name, a voice, and a look that fits together. No patchwork. No guessing. Just a system that's ready to launch.

The outcome is a full brand identity — positioning, visuals, and guidelines your team can use immediately.

PROCESS

01 Project introduction

  • Brief creation
  • Free consultation
  • Clarifying the task, workflow format, and Q&A
  • Defining the goals and objectives the brand must solve
  • Agreement and kick-off after 100% prepayment for the stage

02 Research

  • Strategy session: positioning, values, brand direction
  • Desk research: market, competitors, category language
  • Customer development: interviews with the target audience to test hypotheses and uncover insights

03 Visual direction concept

  • Building a visual idea based on the brand’s meaning and business goals
  • Developing the elements and their logic: color, typography, graphics, form
  • Fixing the system as a cohesive style where all elements work together

04 Design & presentation

  • Packaging the result into a guideline with identity rules and usage recommendations
  • Delivering the guideline in a practical format ready for implementation
