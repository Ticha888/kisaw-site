DESIGN TEAM SUBSCRIPTION

A design team subscription is a flexible way to get consistent, high-quality design work done — without hiring in-house or briefing a new team every time.

You get a dedicated design team that knows your brand, understands your goals, and is ready to execute across any task — from small edits to full campaigns. Fast, reliable, and always on-brand.

The outcome is a steady design workflow that keeps your brand moving forward — with a team that feels like your own.

PROCESS

01 Briefing

  • Getting to know your brand, goals, and existing materials
  • Defining the scope of work and types of tasks covered
  • Aligning on tools, communication format, and response times
  • Agreement and kick-off after 100% prepayment for the first month

02 Kick-off

  • Defining goals, target audience, and tone of voice for social media
  • Developing a content strategy: topics, formats, and posting frequency
  • Building a visual system for social media aligned with the brand
  • Creating a content plan for the first period

03 Project Reporting

  • Managing tasks through CRM: submission, tracking, and delivery
  • Regular approvals and feedback rounds
  • Monthly reports: tasks completed, time spent, results delivered

04 Renewal & Support

  • Review of the month: what worked, what to improve
  • Adjusting scope, formats, or priorities for the next period
  • Seamless renewal — the team stays up to speed, no re-onboarding needed

WHAT YOU GET

Fast turnaround with a clear approval process
Monthly reports and transparent workflow
Easy renewal with no loss of context or quality
